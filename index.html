Meet the Chef




Genni Nakamura


Nakamura-san is a bona fide ramen celebrity. While abroad, she chronicled her experience eating hundreds of bowls of ramen on her blog, Ramen Women. While she has always been a huge anime and manga fan growing up in Tokyo, she burst onto the ramen scene with her food stall pop-up, Otaku Ramen, after realizing her love for otaku culture first hand in Japan, at Anime Japan, one of the largest anime conventions in the world.
Nakamura hopes her food is a love letter to fans everywhere.
